3. Say BYE to your comfort zone: While staying in your comfort 
zone might seem the safest option, it doesn’t always is the best 
decision. Sometimes it is the only thing that discourages you 
from developing or growing, or exploring new ways of thinking. 
Everyone would have heard of the line “best views come from hardest 
climbs”, and it is true that you need to allow yourself to let your 
thoughts flow freely outside the vision you usually have. Remember
 that no one else is judging your thoughts and you shouldn’t either.
